# AV Meeting Room Checklist

Use this checklist before important Teams meetings or when responding to AV support requests.

## Pre-Meeting Room Check

- Confirm the room display powers on.
- Confirm the Logitech camera is detected.
- Confirm microphone input is detected in Teams.
- Confirm speaker output is routed to the room device.
- Confirm the Teams room calendar shows the meeting.
- Confirm HDMI or USB-C screen sharing works.
- Confirm wireless sharing works if supported.
- Confirm remote participants can hear the room clearly.
- Confirm the room can hear remote participants clearly.
- Confirm camera framing is appropriate for the room.

## Fast Troubleshooting Flow

1. Check physical connections: USB, HDMI, power, network.
2. Restart the Teams room device or meeting PC.
3. Confirm the correct camera, microphone, and speaker are selected.
4. Test with a quick Teams call.
5. Check whether the issue affects one meeting or all meetings.
6. Escalate to Level 2 if firmware, driver, device enrollment, or network restrictions appear involved.

## Ticket Notes To Capture

- Room name
- Meeting time and business impact
- Device model
- Teams error message, if any
- Camera, microphone, or speaker affected
- Steps already attempted
- Whether a workaround was provided
