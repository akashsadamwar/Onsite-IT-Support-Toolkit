# Onboarding and Offboarding Runbook

## Onboarding Workflow

1. Receive manager request.
2. Confirm start date, department, location, and required equipment.
3. Prepare laptop, peripherals, and access request.
4. Coordinate AD account, Microsoft 365 license, mailbox, Teams, SharePoint, and distribution groups.
5. Confirm asset assignment.
6. Provide day-one login support.
7. Validate Outlook, Teams, OneDrive, printer, VPN, and required applications.
8. Close ticket with completed checklist and asset tag.

## Offboarding Workflow

1. Receive HR or manager offboarding notification.
2. Confirm final working date and access removal timing.
3. Recover laptop, charger, dock, headset, badge, mobile device, and other assets.
4. Coordinate account disablement and mailbox/data transfer.
5. Document recovered and missing equipment.
6. Remove asset assignment.
7. Reimage or wipe device according to policy.
8. Close or escalate ticket based on missing equipment or access issues.

## Quality Standard

Every onboarding or offboarding ticket should make it clear what was requested, what was completed, what is pending, and who owns the next action.
