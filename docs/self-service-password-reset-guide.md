# Self-Service Password Reset Guide

## Before You Start

Use this guide if you know your current password or have already registered for self-service password reset.

## Steps

1. Go to the company password reset portal.
2. Enter your work email address.
3. Verify your identity using your registered method.
4. Create a new password that meets company requirements.
5. Wait a few minutes for the password to sync.
6. Sign out and sign back in to Microsoft 365 apps.
7. Update saved passwords on your phone and other devices.

## Contact IT If

- You are not registered for self-service reset.
- Your verification method is unavailable.
- You are locked out after multiple attempts.
- Outlook, Teams, or OneDrive keeps asking for the old password.
